Sea Level Rise Map

QGIS Cartography Climate Justice

Using data from the National Oceanic and Atmospheric Administration: National Centers for Environmental Information and the US Census Bureau, this map analyzes the projected sea level rise in Savannah, GA by 2050. The effects of climate change are rapidly intensifying. Coastal cities like Savannah, GA, are among the most at risk.
