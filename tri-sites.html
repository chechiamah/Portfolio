TRI Site Map

GIS EPA Data Climate Justice

Made in 2024, this is a Google My Maps interactive map of Toxic Release Inventory (TRI) Sites in Orange County and their proximity to K-12 schools.

There is a higher presence of TRI sites in lower income areas in the county. This map shed light on this disparity.

Data Sources: Environmental Protection Agency, California Department of Education
